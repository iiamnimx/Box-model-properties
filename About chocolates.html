CHOCOLATES

Candy

Chocolate is a food made from cocoa beans that can be eaten as a candy or used as a flavoring in other foods. It has a rich flavor, is easy to digest, and provides a quick source of energy. Chocolate is also rich in flavanols, antioxidants, and magnesium, which may provide nutritional and therapeutic benefits.

Belgium Chocolate

Belgian chocolate is a world-renowned chocolate product that originated in Belgium and is known for its high quality.Although the raw materials for chocolate come from other countries, Belgium has become a major producer and is associated with the product. The most commonly accepted standard for labeling chocolate as "Belgian" is that it must be produced in Belgium.
